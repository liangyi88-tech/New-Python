Signal framework

How likely volume increase is estimated

1. Hiring volume

Count recent public job postings tied to manufacturing, plant, quality, warehouse, and supply chain functions.

2. Role relevance

Higher weighting for production, maintenance, operations, and packaging-related functions.

3. Recency

Fresh openings are scored more heavily than older postings.

4. Market fit

Singapore and Malaysia locations receive additional lead-priority weighting.

Method

Public-source intelligence, not black-box guessing

This MVP is designed to work with public job pages and scrape-friendly job boards where feasible. It estimates likely manufacturing growth by combining job counts, role types, and location fit.

LinkedIn-style hiring signals are represented through publicly available job activity patterns rather than direct dependence on a private platform.

Scoring inputs

  • Recent manufacturing openings
  • Production and plant role concentration
  • Logistics and warehouse demand
  • Country and city relevance
  • Signal freshness
